Special Treasurelists

Some treasurelists are used for special purposes. These can be found in the subfolders "God Intervention", "Dragon Player Evolution" and "Player Creation". They cannot be used in maps.

The "God Intervention" lists are of special importance. They define which bonuses the Daimonin gods donate to their worshippers. These lists are traveresed from top to bottom and the grace_limit objects are only passed when the worshipping player has a certain amount of grace.
