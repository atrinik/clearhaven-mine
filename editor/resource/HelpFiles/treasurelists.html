Treasurelists

What are Treasurelists?

Whenever a monster is generated or loaded in the world of Daimonin, items are placed in it's inventory according to the monster's treasurelist.
Hence, treasurelists define what items get dropped when the monster is killed. Even more importantly, they define which skills and spells the monster has at it's disposal.

You can view the treasurelists by selecting menu "Resources->View Treasurelists", or from the attribute dialog.
